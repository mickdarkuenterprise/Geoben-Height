Our Property

Geoben Height is situated on Helium Drive in the Asofan New Station area of Accra, Ghana — a well-connected location that makes it easy to explore the city while retreating to a quiet, comfortable space at the end of the day.

We offer three types of accommodation — a Single Room, Chamber & Hall and a Two-Bedroom Suite — designed to serve a wide range of guests, from solo travellers on business to families visiting loved ones in the city.

"A clean, secure, and welcoming space — whether you are here for a night or a month."

Our Commitment

At Geoben Height, we believe that comfortable accommodation should not come at an unreasonable cost. Every room is kept spotlessly clean, fully air-conditioned, and equipped with kitchen facilities so guests can enjoy the freedom of home-cooked meals during their stay.

We take pride in providing genuine Ghanaian hospitality — warm, attentive, and personalised. Our team is available around the clock to ensure your stay is as smooth as possible.

Location & Accessibility

Our Helium Drive address puts guests within easy reach of Accra's key areas. Asofan New Station is a vibrant, well-serviced part of the city with access to public transport, local markets, restaurants, and amenities.

Whether you are new to Accra or a returning visitor, Geoben Height provides the perfect base to make the most of your time in the city.

How to Reach Us

Bookings can be made via our online form, by phone, or through WhatsApp on 020 751 5584. You can also email us at geobenheight@gmail.com. We aim to confirm all reservations within 24 hours.
